The garden apps

Every prompt is read out loud, so pre-readers play on their own. Pick an animal, earn stars, and pick up right where you left off — with a take-turns mode for siblings.

✨

Little Lights

Ages 2–6 · a magic garden at night

Colors and their meanings, the seven rainbow body lights, plants that grow above and below the ground, calm breathing, and a trip through space — with read-along stories where every word bounces as it's spoken.

Play Little Lights →
📖

Bible Buddies

Ages 2–6 · a sunny garden of scripture

A tappable bookshelf of 89 books — the world's biggest Bible — plus counting games, hero quizzes, heart lessons, and eight narrated stories. Uses Yah, Yehoshua HaMashiach, and Hebrew names, teaching English names as kids advance.

Play Bible Buddies →
🎈

Pop & Play

Ages 1–2 · tap, hear, collect

The littlest one's first app: things rain gently down the screen — letters, animals, foods — and every tap pops them with confetti and says the word out loud. Taps earn stickers for their very own sticker scene. No menus, no reading needed.
